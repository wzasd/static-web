Summary

Since then, annual online insurance sales had grown from $110 million to $0.9 billion in 2019.

Smart Risk Assessment

In a chat session with an intelligent consultant, 10 questions will quickly help the system analyze the customer's health status and potential risks.

customers can also obtain product-related information and questions on the chat page.

Risk Report

New Product Interface

It is important to display a simple but clear design on the mobile phone. In the banners, I chose colors with low saturation that cater to young people.

The eye-catching wide banner at the top shows the insurance features, coverage, and rates, and helps customers quickly obtain key information.

A fixed purchase button at the bottom allows users to initiate a purchase at any time.
